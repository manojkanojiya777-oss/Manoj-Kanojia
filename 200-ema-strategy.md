# 200 EMA Pullback Strategy

## Strategy Objective
To trade in the direction of the long-term trend using pullbacks to the 200 EMA.

---

## Market Condition
- Only trade in trending markets
- Avoid sideways or choppy markets

---

## Setup Rules

### Buy Setup
- Price above 200 EMA
- Price pulls back towards EMA
- Bullish candle confirmation near EMA

### Sell Setup (optional)
- Price below 200 EMA
- Pullback towards EMA
- Bearish confirmation candle

---

## Entry Rules
- Enter after confirmation candle closes
- Avoid entering in the middle of a move

---

## Stop Loss
- Below recent swing low (for buy)
- Above recent swing high (for sell)

---

## Target
- Minimum Risk:Reward = 1:2
- Trail profits if trend is strong

---

## Risk Management
- Risk per trade: 1-2% of capital
- Maximum 2 trades per day

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Trading in sideways market
- Ignoring trend direction
- Overtrading
- Emotional decisions
